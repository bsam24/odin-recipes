Smoked Brisket Recipe

Description

This recipe for Texas Style Smoked Beef Brisket is a process for melt-in-your-mouth smoked brisket.

You won't find delicious, tender, juicy, meat anywhere else in the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 12-14 pound whole packer brisket
  • 2 Tablespoons coarse Kosher salt
  • 2 Tablespoons coarse ground black pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons garlic powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Select your brisket
  2. Trim the brisket
  3. Season your brisket
  4. Get smoking
  5. Wrap the brisket
  6. Rest your smoked brisket
  7. Slice your brisket
  8. Serve your gorgeously smoked brisket
